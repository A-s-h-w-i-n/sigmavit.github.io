ABOUT

Sigma Xi: The Scientific Research Honor Society is a non-profit honor society for scientists and engineers which was founded in 1886 at Cornell University by a junior faculty member and a handful of graduate students.

The Art of Research and Publishing. Sigma Xi conducted an orientation session for students interested in the extracurricular course titled "The Art of Research and Publishing". The session lasted for two hours and the introduction was provided by the chapter's faculty coordinator - Dr. Akella Sivaramakrishna. The students attending the orientation were motivated by the speaker to solve real world issues by developing solutions using scientific methods. The orientation was well received and the attendees found it very useful.
